Newcomer Cup

The Newcomer Cup will start with the Group stage. There are three groups. The first two places in each groups and the best two third places will enter the quarterfinal. Then there will be the knockout stage. The game is seven-a-side. The game lasts for 50 minutes, 25 minutes for each half and there is a 10 minutes break between.

Knockout stage

School of Material

Our team secured the second place in our group and entered the quarterfinal. Our first opponent was team from School of Material. We had a 4:2 victory entering the semifinal of the Newcomer Cup.


School of Mechanical Engineering

Our opponent had a strong defence so we could not make a score given many shots. We also had good defence for the whole game except a moment we just made the replacement. Our opponent caught the chance and make a wonderful heading through corner. We were eliminated.


Paris High-Tech Engineer Institute

Our opponent for consolation is Paris High-Tech Engineer Institute. They scored a penalty kick in the very early stage of the game. In the second half, one in our teams got his second yellow card because he complained about the referees. But our captain Tong scored an equalizer at the last ten seconds of the injury time. We managed to drag the game into penalty shootout even with six people. I made the penalty kick for the third round and the eighth round. Our opponent missed the eighth one. We finally got the third place in NewCommer Cup.
