Hope Cup

The Hope Cup will start with the Group stage. There are 3 groups. The first four places in group A and B can enter the knockout stage. The last places in group A and B will have a game and the lose side will have a game with the first place in group C as the promotion and relegation match. The winner will be in the level A for next season and the loser will be in the level B for the next season.


Personal Experience
