Hope Cup

The Hope Cup will start with the Group stage. There are 3 groups. The first four places in group A and B can enter the knockout stage. The last places in group A and B will have a game and the lose side will have a game with the first place in group C as the promotion and relegation match. The winner will be in the level A for next season and the loser will be in the level B for the next season.


Personal Experience

I entered the department team after our journey as the second runner-up in 2018 Newcomer Cup. I got the chance as the substitute of left-back in the game vs Agriculture and Design Joint Team on April 2018. The Hope Cup in 2019 was largely affected by the pandemic and I start to play the substitute right-back in the Fall of 2021 and we got the sixth place in Hope Cup from 2019 - 2020.

Department of Material
