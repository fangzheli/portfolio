Main tournaments

There are two main tournaments for students to attend. The first one is the Newcomer Cup, which is a seven-a-side soccer game that starts at the beginning of October and ends at the end of Novmber. Only freshmen can enroll in this event. The team is built based on departments. The second event is the most important soccer tournament in SJTU called Hope Cup, which is a eleven-a-side soccer match. The team is also built based on departments. The group stage usually begins at the beginning of October and ends at the end of April. The knockout stage begins at the beginning of May and ends at the end of June.
